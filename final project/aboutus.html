About Us

Our Story Body Building Training Studio aims to bring ‘next generation fitness’ with top-notch specialized workout facilities and training programs for all members. SLAM, widely regarded as one of India’s premium fitness destinations, is more than just a gym; it’s a way of life. The vision and mission of our venture are to provide the best fitness experience possible under the supervision of highly qualified and experienced professionals. We meet the demand for an upscale gym in the growing suburb of Anna Nagar and Redhills areas
